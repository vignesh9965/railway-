WELCOME TO INDIAN RAILWAYS RESERVATION SYSTEM

Indian Railways (IR) is a government agency under the ownership of Ministry of Railways that operates India's national railway system.
It manages the fourth-largest railway network in the world by size, with a route length of 67,956 km (42,226 mi) .
